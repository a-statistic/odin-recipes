Korean Bulgolgi Bowl

Home Page

A popular Korean dish

bulgolgi is a delicious Korean meat that goes well with rice

This recipe will take only 15 minutes to prepare, another 15 minutes to cook and serves 4 people

In the interest of time, I am not actually creating a whole web page for this recipe or any other. They can be the actual recipes can be found at allrecipes.com

Ingredients (only top 5 because there are tooooo many)

  • Beef
  • rice
  • Brown sugar
  • soy sauce
  • lettuce

Steps

  1. Cook the beef
  2. Cook the rice
  3. add soy sauce to beef
  4. put lettuce on rice
  5. put beef on lettuce
