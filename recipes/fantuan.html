Fantaun

Home Page

This is my personal favorite. It is a dish local to Taiwan. Fan, meaning rice, and tuan, meaning ball This is exactly that, but the taste is so much more. If I had to pic only one food the eat the rest of my life, it probably wouldn't be this, but I would consider it briefly.

Fantaun is a popular breakfast dish in Taiwan that can easily be found in the open air markets or small breakfast shops literally everywhere. If you go, you need to try it! Seriously. Or the next best thing, find this real recipe online and make it.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Youtiao
  • pickled raddish
  • minced meat of your choosing
  • corn

Steps

  1. Make the rice, its gotta be the sticky kind or it wont work
  2. smash the rice out kinda flat
  3. Lay all the ingredients into it
  4. pick up the delicious mess with a hot wet rag
  5. using the rag, press it all neatly into a tight ball, the ingredients should all be inside of the rice
